How It Works

📄

DA Price Index

Daily commodity prices from the Department of Agriculture — vegetables, meat, fish, rice, and more from Kamuning, Nepa-Q Mart, and other markets.

🥘

Panlasang Pinoy Recipes

Recipes from panlasangpinoy.com — scraped for ingredients, instructions, servings, and cook time. Ingredients are matched to DA commodity prices.

📊

FNRI Nutrition Data

Nutritional values from the FNRI Food Composition Table — the Philippine standard. Calories, protein, vitamins, minerals per 100g for each ingredient.

Browse Filipino Recipes

Filter and sort by nutrition, price, or specific produce. Nutrition & cost are estimated from latest DA prices + FNRI data.

Tags are computed from each dish's actual ingredients and per-serving nutrition — not hand-labeled.
